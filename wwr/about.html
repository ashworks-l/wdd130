History

Splash Company started floating the river in 1995 and has grown with a strong commitment to unforgettable outdoor experiences. For more than thirty years, our family-owned and operated business has focused on providing quality service, adventure, and safety, ensuring every guest enjoys memorable moments on the river with confidence and excitement.

We have a team of experienced guides who are fully dedicated to providing you with the best experience possible. Safety, fun, and adventure remain at the heart of everything we do, ensuring every trip becomes exciting, meaningful, and unforgettable for everyone who joins us on the river to explore its natural beauty.
